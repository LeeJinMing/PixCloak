PixCloak – Local Image Compression & Redaction (Open-source)
One-liner: Compress and redact images locally in your browser – no upload, no tracking. Works offline (PWA).
Highlights: Target KB, Resize Longest/Exact, JPEG/WebP/PNG, Batch ZIP, Rename, Solid/Pixelate Redaction, EXIF/GPS removal.
